Why We're Building Bill Tracker

By Mark 8/4/15

Community dialog and insight into pending legislation

Data shows that legislators don't always listen to or care about what constituents want. This is a real breakdown in democracy.

Although there is transparency in legislation, not everyone knows where to find pending legislation or has time to follow it. It's often not presented in a way that fosters dialog. We built Bill Tracker to try to remedy that and to crowdsource analysis of pending legislation.
